Ok About Me

I am excited to be a part of the new online Master of Science program: GIS & Web Map Programming at UW-Madison. Now I have the opportunity to learn some of the latest open source GIS software and advance my web based GIS developments skills. Check out my digital portfolio. Bootstrap is used to create the responsive web pages.

My background includes working at an Electric & Gas utility, the Wisconsin Department of Transportation, and City of Milwaukee. I have over 25 years of GIS experience that includes an extensive background in Esri applications including: SDE, ArcMap and more recently ArcGIS Server and Portal for ArcGIS.

I am involved in developing client server applications and providing troubleshooting and support of our GIS applications. I also have a lot of experience working with multiple workgroups while coordinating complex technical projects that include software upgrades and implementing new applications that interface with GIS.

I am always looking for new ways to use GIS and spatial technology to solve problems and make it easier for people to do their jobs.
